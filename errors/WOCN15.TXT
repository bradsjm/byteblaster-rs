==DISCUSSION==

A COMBINATION OF SNOW, ICE PELLETS, FREEZING RAIN, AND RAIN IS
EXPECTED.

TIME SPAN: WEDNESDAY AFTERNOON UNTIL THURSDAY AFTERNOON.

POTENTIAL SNOW AND ICE PELLET AMOUNTS: POSSIBLY REACHING 15 CM.

POTENTIAL FREEZING RAIN DURATION: NEAR 4 HOURS.

REMARKS: A LOW PRESSURE SYSTEM IS EXPECTED TO BRING A VARIETY OF
WINTRY PRECIPITATION ON WEDNESDAY INTO THURSDAY. THERE IS THE
POTENTIAL FOR A PROLONGED PERIOD OF FREEZING RAIN OVERNIGHT ON
WEDNESDAY. A TRANSITION TO RAIN IS EXPECTED BY THURSDAY MORNING.

ACCUMULATIONS OF ICE MAY CREATE SLIPPERY AND HAZARDOUS TRAVEL OR
WALKING CONDITIONS. ICE BUILDUP ON TREES AND UTILITY INFRASTRUCTURE
MAY CAUSE DAMAGE OR OUTAGES.

PLAN AHEAD IN CASE SCHOOLS OR SCHEDULED ACTIVITIES ARE CANCELLED.
CONSIDER ADJUSTING TRAVEL PLANS TO AVOID THE MOST ADVERSE
CONDITIONS.
