==DISCUSSION==

A COMBINATION OF SNOW, ICE PELLETS, FREEZING RAIN, AND RAIN IS
EXPECTED.

TIME SPAN: WEDNESDAY EVENING UNTIL THURSDAY AFTERNOON.

POTENTIAL SNOW AND ICE PELLET AMOUNTS: POSSIBLY REACHING 15 CM.

POTENTIAL FREEZING RAIN DURATION: NEAR 4 HOURS.

REMARKS: A LOW PRESSURE SYSTEM IS EXPECTED TO BRING A VARIETY OF
WINTRY PRECIPITATION WEDNESDAY EVENING INTO THURSDAY. THERE REMAINS
SOME UNCERTAINTY IN THE EXACT TRACK OF THIS SYSTEM BUT THERE IS
POTENTIAL FOR SIGNIFICANT AMOUNTS OF SNOW AND ICE PELLETS AS WELL AS
A PROLONGED PERIOD OF FREEZING RAIN.

ACCUMULATIONS OF SNOW AND ICE MAY CREATE SLIPPERY AND HAZARDOUS
TRAVEL OR WALKING CONDITIONS. ICE BUILDUP ON TREES AND UTILITY
INFRASTRUCTURE MAY CAUSE DAMAGE OR OUTAGES.

PLAN AHEAD IN CASE SCHOOLS OR SCHEDULED ACTIVITIES ARE CANCELLED.
CONSIDER ADJUSTING TRAVEL PLANS TO AVOID THE MOST ADVERSE
CONDITIONS.
